Final Project


"The Swear Jar"



This is my Final Project, where i came up with a potential art piece that would utilize machine learning to make a visual interpretation of the use of swear words in social media postings. i was inspired by other "big data" art pieces that are becoming common. i also drew from the pervasive nature of social media, and how our conversations on it impacts our ways of thinking, and often our sense of morality and propriety.
